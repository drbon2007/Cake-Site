Baking Tips & Tricks
Master the art of baking with these essential techniques.
🌡️

Room Temperature Ingredients

Always bring butter, eggs and dairy to room temperature before mixing for a smoother batter.

⚖️

Weigh Your Flour

Using a kitchen scale instead of cups ensures consistent results every single time.

🔥

Know Your Oven

Ovens vary by ±20°C. Use an oven thermometer and rotate pans halfway through baking.

🧈

Don't Overmix

Over-beating develops gluten and leads to dense, tough cakes. Mix just until combined.

❄️

Cool Completely

Never frost a warm cake. Let it cool fully on a wire rack to prevent melting buttercream.

🍳

Grease Your Tins Well

Line with parchment and butter for a clean release every time — no broken cakes!
